Data Scientist

I am a data scientist and researcher specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I completed my Master of Science (Thesis-based M.Sc.) in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence from Lakehead University, Canada, and earned my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (formerly West Bengal University of Technology), India.

My work primarily focuses on Deep Learning, Data Engineering, and Optimization Algorithms. I enjoy building scalable systems that can transform raw data into meaningful, actionable insights. Over the years, I have worked on a wide range of projects, including Brain-Computer Interfaces, Image Processing, Metaheuristic-based Data Clustering, and real-time data pipelines.
